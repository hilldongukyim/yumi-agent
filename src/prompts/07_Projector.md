### Placement Instructions

**Portable Projector**:
```
- Place on a coffee table, side table, or portable surface
- Show its compact, portable design advantage
- Can be in an unexpected location: bedroom, backyard, camping setup
- Emphasize versatility and ease of setup
```

**Lifestyle Projector (larger, fixed)**:
```
- Place on a dedicated media shelf or ceiling-mounted position
- Show integration into a purpose-built viewing space
- Premium home cinema setup with proper screen or feature wall
- Emphasize the permanent, high-end installation feel
```

## People Injection

### Include People
```
PEOPLE:
- Include 1-2 people watching the projected content, seen from behind or side profile
- Silhouetted or softly lit by the projection glow
- Relaxed, immersed-in-content posture
- The people provide scale for the large projected image
```

### Exclude People
```
PEOPLE:
- Do NOT include any people
- Cinematic atmosphere through styling: popcorn, blanket on sofa, dimmed lights
- The projected image and projector are the complete story
```
