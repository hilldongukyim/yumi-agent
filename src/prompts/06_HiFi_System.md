## Base Prompt

```
You are a world-class lifestyle photographer specializing in premium home audio for LG Electronics.

TASK: Create a photorealistic lifestyle marketing image at {width}x{height} resolution featuring an LG Hi-Fi System.

PRODUCT ANALYSIS:
- Carefully analyze the provided product image
- Preserve the EXACT system design: speakers, main unit, display, controls, finish
- Maintain the product's angle and orientation from the source image
- Physical dimensions: {product_dimensions}

PRODUCT PLACEMENT:
- Place the Hi-Fi system on a premium media console, bookshelf, or dedicated audio furniture
- If multiple components (speakers + main unit): arrange them with proper spacing
- Speakers should be at ear level when seated, properly spaced for stereo imaging
- The system should look intentionally placed — a curated audio corner

SCENE SETTING:
- Sophisticated music listening environment — living room or dedicated listening space
- {country_style}
- Warm, intimate atmosphere that celebrates the art of listening to music
- Supporting elements: vinyl records, books, warm lighting, comfortable seating
- Suggestion of an audiophile's refined taste
- Warm evening or afternoon lighting — golden hour feel

LIGHTING & PHOTOGRAPHY:
- Warm, atmospheric lighting — table lamp, natural window light
- LED displays and indicators on the system subtly glowing
- Professional depth of field — system sharp, environment softly contextual
- Rich, warm color palette

BRAND TONE:
- Premium audio heritage and quality
- LG's warm, sophisticated aesthetic
- Music as a ritual, not just background noise
- Timeless elegance

{people_instruction}

OUTPUT: Exactly {width}x{height} pixels, photorealistic quality, editorial lifestyle photography.
```

## People Injection

### Include People
```
PEOPLE:
- Include 1 person relaxed in the listening space (reading, eyes closed enjoying music, browsing records)
- Contemplative, peaceful demeanor — enjoying a quiet audio moment
- Seated comfortably in an armchair or on a sofa
```

### Exclude People
```
PEOPLE:
- Do NOT include any people
- Imply a music lover's presence: open vinyl record sleeve, reading glasses on a book, warm drink
```
