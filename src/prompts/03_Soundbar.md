# Soundbar - Lifestyle Prompt

## Category Info
- **ID**: `soundbar`
- **Keywords**: soundbar, sound bar, home cinema, home theater, all-in-one soundbar, subwoofer, sound suite
- **Typical Placement**: Below TV on media console, wall-mounted under TV, or standalone on shelf
- **Subcategories**: Home Cinema Soundbar, Soundbar with Subwoofer, All-in-one Soundbar, Sound Suite

- Ensure the soundbar is clearly visible and not obscured by other objects

LIGHTING & PHOTOGRAPHY:
- Moody, cinematic lighting — warm evening tones

---

## People Injection

### Include People
```
PEOPLE:
- Include 1-2 people on a sofa/couch, enjoying content (movie night feel)
- Shown from behind or at an angle — focused on the screen/soundbar
- Relaxed, comfortable posture — blanket, popcorn, or drinks optional
- The people set the scene but the soundbar remains the product focus
```

### Exclude People
```
PEOPLE:
- Do NOT include any people
- Convey the cinematic atmosphere through set design: popcorn bowl, dimmed lights, cozy blanket draped on sofa
- The empty scene should feel like someone just stepped away momentarily
```

---

## Product Angle Awareness

```
CRITICAL — PRODUCT ANGLE PRESERVATION:
- Analyze the soundbar's angle in the source image (front view, slight angle, 3/4 view)
- Reproduce this same viewing angle in the lifestyle scene
- The soundbar's position on the console should match the source orientation
- Ensure the TV above (if shown) is at a complementary angle
```
