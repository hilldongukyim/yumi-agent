### Mount Type Instructions

**Stand Mount (기본)**:
```
- Place the TV on a sleek media console or TV stand
- The stand/feet of the TV must be clearly visible and properly resting on the surface
- Console should complement the TV's design language
- Allow adequate spacing around the TV for a premium, uncluttered look
```

**Wall Mount**:
```
- Mount the TV flush on the wall with minimal gap
- No visible mounting hardware — clean, seamless wall integration
- Position at optimal viewing height (eye level when seated)
- The wall area around the TV should be clean with optional subtle decor (floating shelves, art pieces to one side)
```
