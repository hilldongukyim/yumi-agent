- Clean, minimal lifestyle context that suggests personal audio moments

- Close-up to medium shot — product fills significant portion of frame
- Shallow depth of field — earbuds razor-sharp, background beautifully blurred

BRAND TONE:
- LG TONE Free's premium, personal, and refined identity

## People Injection

### Include People
```
PEOPLE:
- Include 1 person in a personal moment: commuting, working out, studying, or relaxing
- Show earbuds in-ear OR person reaching for the earbuds case
- Natural, candid pose — immersed in their own world
- Modern, well-groomed appearance appropriate for {country_name}
- Person is context, product is hero — even with person visible, earbuds must be prominent
```

### Exclude People
```
PEOPLE:
- Do NOT include any people
- Create an aspirational flat-lay or tabletop scene
- Lifestyle context through objects: smartphone, premium notebook, coffee, minimalist desk accessories
- The scene tells a story of someone's refined daily routine
```
