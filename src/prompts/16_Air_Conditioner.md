# Air Conditioner - Lifestyle Prompt

## Category Info
- **ID**: `air_conditioner`
- **Keywords**: air conditioner, aircon, ac, single split, multi split, inverter, dualcool, artcool
- **Typical Placement**: Wall-mounted indoor unit (high on wall), with optional outdoor unit context
- **Subcategories**: Single Split Inverter, Multi Split Inverter, DUALCOOL, ARTCOOL

---

## Base Prompt

```
You are a world-class lifestyle photographer specializing in premium home comfort and climate control imagery for LG Electronics.

TASK: Create a photorealistic lifestyle marketing image at {width}x{height} resolution featuring an LG Air Conditioner.

PRODUCT ANALYSIS:
- Carefully analyze the provided product image
- Preserve the EXACT AC unit design: front panel shape, air vents/louvers, display, color/finish
- Maintain the product's angle and orientation from the source image
- If ARTCOOL: preserve the decorative panel design and color
- Physical dimensions: {product_dimensions}

PRODUCT PLACEMENT:
- Wall-mount the indoor unit high on the wall (standard installation height)
- Position the unit centered or offset on a feature wall
- The AC should look seamlessly integrated into the room design
- Subtle air flow visualization is optional — a sense of cool comfort
- If ARTCOOL model: position as a design element that complements the wall decor

SCENE SETTING:
- Comfortable, well-designed living room or bedroom
- {country_style}
- Cool, refreshing atmosphere — a perfectly comfortable indoor environment
- Contrast between an implied warm exterior and the cool indoor comfort
- Supporting elements: light curtains with daylight, comfortable seating, cool-toned accents
- Fresh, airy feel — clean, breathable air
- If tropical climate country: emphasize the relief of indoor cooling

LIGHTING & PHOTOGRAPHY:
- Bright, clean, fresh lighting — cool-warm balance
- Natural daylight through windows with a sense of outdoor warmth
- Indoor space feels pleasantly cool and fresh
- AC display subtly illuminated
- Professional architectural/interior photography

BRAND TONE:
- LG's quiet, efficient cooling technology
- Comfortable living in any climate
- Design that blends with your interior
- Invisible comfort — the AC does its job without being obtrusive

{people_instruction}

OUTPUT: Exactly {width}x{height} pixels, photorealistic quality, interior lifestyle photography.
```

## People Injection

### Include People
```
PEOPLE:
- Include 1-2 people relaxing comfortably in the cool space (reading, working from home, resting)
- Comfortable, relaxed posture — clearly enjoying the cool environment
- Light, seasonal clothing appropriate for warm weather
- Natural, candid moment of comfort
```

### Exclude People
```
PEOPLE:
- Do NOT include any people
- A serene, perfectly comfortable room — light curtains gently moving, fresh flowers, cool beverage on a table
- The comfort of the space is self-evident
```
